Cherry Blossom of

Meghalaya

Nestled in the serene hills of Northeast India, Meghalaya transforms into a picturesque wonderland during the Cherry Blossom Festival.

The capital city, Shillong, and its surrounding areas become the epicenter of this floral celebration. Visitors are greeted with avenues adorned with blooming cherry trees, creating a mesmerizing tunnel of petals.

Meenakshi Temple of

Madurai

In the heart of the historic city of Madurai stands the grand Meenakshi Amman Temple, a testament to Tamil Nadu's rich cultural heritage and architectural brilliance.

Dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar (a form of Lord Shiva), this temple is not just a place of worship but a vibrant cultural hub.

Kumbh Mela of

Prayagraj

The Kumbh Mela, held in the sacred city of Prayagraj, is one of the largest and most awe-inspiring spiritual gatherings in the world.

This grand festival, celebrated every twelve years, draws millions of devotees, ascetics, and tourists from across the globe to the banks of the holy confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati rivers.
